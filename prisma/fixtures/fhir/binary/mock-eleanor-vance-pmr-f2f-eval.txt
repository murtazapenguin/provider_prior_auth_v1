Step 2 — Walker: NOT FEASIBLE. Patient cannot stand independently. Walker requires intact lower-extremity weight-bearing capacity, which the patient does not have. Even with a rollator, balance and lower-extremity weakness preclude safe use within the home.

Step 3 — Manual wheelchair: INADEQUATE. Patient currently uses a manual wheelchair (K0001, dispensed 2018). Step 4 — Power-operated vehicle (POV / scooter, K0800-K0812): NOT APPROPRIATE. Step 5 — Power wheelchair Group 2 (K0820-K0843): INADEQUATE for this patient's needs. Group 2 PWCs lack the power-tilt feature that is medically necessary for this patient given her trunk weakness, pressure-ulcer risk (BMI 24, sedentary >18 hr/day), and need for repositioning to manage spasticity.

Step 6 — Power wheelchair Group 3 with single power option (K0856) + power tilt (K0108): MEDICALLY NECESSARY. The patient requires a Group 3 PWC for the following reasons:
  - Permanent neurological diagnosis (MS) consistent with CMS National Coverage Determination criteria for Group 3 PMD.
